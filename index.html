My Journey with my God

January, 2022

BC, Canada

To God be the glory. For He is good and His goodness reigns forever. LORD I want to thank You for Your patience and Your Love that You have shown upon me. I cannot express how grateful I am. What you have seen for my life, that is more than what I could ever have planned with my limited understanding. Thank you Jesus. I Love You.

I have been born to a beautiful Message Believing wonderful Christian parents. I have 5 siblings I am the third. By the grace of God, we all have received Jesus Christ into our Life as our Personal Saviour.My life had always been so different and extraordinary as I see it. How He led me, and worked in my life was so sweet and always filled with His Love. After my graduation when I got my first job, I never did realize God left me alone. There was always someone like a mentor or a guardian God kept in person to take care of me. But it was when I moved to Mumbai at my second job that God dealt with me. How I came into a beautiful fellowship with Him when I was alone in my room and I used to commune with Him. I see it how God dealt with Moses while  taking His servant away from everyone to deal with Him personally.

In Mumbai, it struck me when I had no-one physically with me except few people around me, who helped me through my day to day stuff.  But it was the absolute quietness in my room when God dealt with me to know Him and how I desired that I want to know Him personally. No-one can fill His place in my life. It's Him alone who is my First Love and by His Grace and Love outpouring I can spend life with every friend and family and people He brings my way. 

It was a beautiful time when I spent time with God, reading His Word, listening to his Voice, a prayerful life, writing letters, fellowshipping with my beautiful sister and her family every weekend.

But when we get so close to Jesus so full of love with Him like you Love Him so completely then comes the testing time and the devil used my weakness, the knowledge of good and evil to try out to go out and see why there are so many good so-called Christians doing good works and so in sweet love with everyone they meet and especially in Message believing churches such difference and hatred to not cooperate even talk with people going to another church. Where was that love spoken in the Bible when people in the Message churches differentiated because of some doctrines or some behaviour and laws and order of church.

It was hypocritical to understand all things and much later was the question was brother Branham truly a prophet sent of God. This doubt as the devil puts, no matter what the truth, its the Truth of God that sets you free. All this spiritual battle made me so bitter to listen to any man's opinion but to directly hear from God Himself. (Again God speaks through man)

After my struggle and desire to know the Truth, God one day gave me a beautiful dream of the Rapture, that He is coming over a Cloud taking me Home while all others whom He do not want is going straight to a big ball of everlasting fire. It is such a scary terrible place. But Praise God, He is never late. He is always on Time.

Back after my struggle with my battles , there came the time when I got to actually continuously listen and read through the Message Books and especially the Supernatural Life of Brother Branham audiobook.

Brother Branham lived such a humble life, being so blessed and powerfully used by God, yet always visioned for the Glory of God to fulfill His Works.His vision to prepare the Bride of Christ, what a love story. Can there be another Love so pure and so Holy but what God has for His Little Bride. The more I ate the unadulterated Word, the more confirmation and faith to be at the Word.

It's such a great feeling to be in Love with Jesus Christ and most importantly to know who you are, what you are here for on earth, to be the LIGHT OF THE WORLD, to be the SALT OF THE EARTH, TO LIVE THE WORD, TO BE THE WORD BRIDE JESUS CHRIST HIMSELF

In Christ we are Perfect. There is no goodness in me, and yet when you give God your least, your sincere desire to serve Him, He can use you mightily.

It was my time in Mumbai, that it happened when the covid struck and every part of the world just went blank on how to function. Churches closed, shops and transport lockdown, no food, no groceries, no means to keep our jobs and security and most damaging to not be able to have fellowship with fellow believers.

There is just something about having fellowship with Godly friends and family discussions when its just Love outpouring, God Himself present amongst us covering us in His embrace.

It was my birthday on the 17th of January, 2020 when I deeply needed a purpose and confirmation from God that He sees my desire to know Him and to be in use for Him. Thats when He gave me the Scripture Ruth 2:12, which says - "The LORD recompense thy work, and a full reward be given thee of the Lord God of Israel, under whose wings thou art come to trust." I know this was my personal moment with my God, because He knew I trust Him with my everything. I could either rely on my limited understanding and try to fix everything which I know how terribly would fail or I would trust my Lord Jesus Christ, who is the Truth, the Way and the Life. He is the Wisest of All. He is the Ancient of Days and He loves me before the foundation of the world.

People and situations can break my heart, my feelings and emotions can twist my mind, but its God's Unfailing Love that sustains me till the end. Now after this verse and Promise from God, I had to know what is my full reward which He promised to give me. And during those times of covid outbreak started the blessed youth without borders meetings by Rapturing Faith Believers Fellowship family from New Zealand and the subsequent streaming of past youth meetings in Cloverdale Bibleway camps. The believers from New Zealand, my family, my amazing friends God gave me from different parts of the world in the Bride and the ministers God used tremendously with His gifts to speak to me personally and directly for my situation I was in. It was so personal and so alive every meeting. Its such a presence like we were worshipping together the Lord Jesus Christ so heavenly atmosphere and I was present in that atmosphere. Just towards the end of the year was the last service where the message came on my full reward and it was God's Amazing love for me. That was my full reward. He Loves me like no other and He loves me so I am able to love others, His Love changes me deeply and it is His Love again that I trust and I know He wont let me down ever, He wont let me be ashamed because I am His and He is mine. How blessed to be chosen by Him. To know Him is truly Life Eternal.

And then the next year 2021, January 17 arrives with yet another expectation from God. How wonderful are His promises, when it teaches you to develop your character after every victory of your old nature, your old habits and here God promised me yet another powerful Scripture from I peter 5:10 which reads "But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto His Eternal Glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you." That was so powerful for me because of the to settle me. After my professional life I knew God used my work and experience in so powerful ways to reach out to people to be a good listener to be a nice friend, to develop my nature to understand and tolerate the differences among different individuals and to truly live as a real Christian where Christ is reflected.

It was truly a journey where God moved me from place to place and then I know God would definitely want a future for me to be settled in. But i know of my imperfections and shortcomings. I know how I lack so many things but its God who does what He wills and here is where I somehow wondered where to next Lord. Will you please take me to them where You want to actively use me for Your Kingdom, where I am not confused but where my heart goes with Your heart, where my Will is not mine, but it becomes to fulfill Your Perfect Will. And it was only God who when He makes the Promise He will always fulfill no matter how delayed it may seem in our eyes but its always God's Perfect timing and He is always on Time. If God does not answer you right quickly may be it is How He wants to speak with you when you get alone with Him to listen to His Voice and follow His Leading and in whatever you do, do it all for the Glory of God. It took one year for me almost but God actually moved me to my dream country Canada where there is snowfall, there is a way to express our point of views on what we think or say, there is respect to our opinions, there is no barging into our privacy and most importantly where I would always want to visit. God knows our heart and he says, Delight in the Lord and He shall fulfill the desires of your heart.

How God brought me here, was all God, how He brought the right people to encourage and guide and assist me with my IELTS exam, my visa application, with getting my offer letter from the only polytechnic university in British Columbia, how He gave me friends and relatives to not feel alone and how He blessed me with going to Cloverdale Bibleway to meet the brothers and sisters I used to stream on the church website back in India. How I got help from people I never knew but God knew. How He gave me favor in their hearts who cared for me and supported me. Oh how good He is. how He blessed me with my much loved family, my mother, my father, my grandma, my siblings, my church and pastors that God gave me in India. The time I got to spend with them. How He never never left me alone. Even today He meets all my needs and How I love to serve Him and live a humble life like Daniel, like Joseph, like brother Branham and fulfill His desire for my life.

It is truly a testimony filled with gratitude for its all God to exalt His Holy Name and Praise be to His Holy Name forever. Whatever God does is for a purpose, so I come with great expectations to what the Lord has in store for me. He does not need me and yet He wants me so I give Him full control of my life and pray to be used by Him as He pleases. Praise be to God. AMEN.

Ruth Juli Khosla
